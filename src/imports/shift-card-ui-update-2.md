Improve shift card layout and employee origin badges.

1. Keep the break indicator on its own line.

The break information (☕ 30хв) should remain below the department/section line.

Do not place the break indicator on the same line as the progress bar.

This keeps the layout visually balanced and improves readability.

Final card structure:

Line 1
Shift time + marketplace indicator

Line 2
Department / section

Line 3
Break information (☕ 30хв)

Line 4
Progress bar

---

2. Differentiate employee origin badges.

Employees working in another department or another store should have visually different badges.

Another department (same store):
Blue badge with the department name.

Example:
Каса

Another store:
Purple badge with the store name.

Example:
Сільпо №124

This allows managers to quickly distinguish internal vs external employees.

---

3. Keep the shift marketplace indicator.

Shifts originating from the marketplace should display a small marketplace icon.

Placement:
Top-right corner of the shift card.

The icon should be subtle and not visually dominant.

It represents the origin of the shift rather than an alert.
