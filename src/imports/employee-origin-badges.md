Add origin indicators for employees who are not штатні for the current department and demonstrate them directly in the schedule.

1. Employee origin badge.

If an employee belongs to another department or another store, display a small origin badge next to the role information in the employee row.

Placement:

Employee name  
Role · workload   [origin badge]

Example:

Ірина Петренко  
Пекар · 100%   [Каса]

Badges should be small and visually secondary.

Style:
font-size: 11–12px  
padding: 2px 6px  
border-radius: 4px

Color mapping:
another department → blue  
another store → purple  
shift marketplace → orange

Badges should not look like alerts or warnings.

2. Demonstration cases in the schedule.

Add two example employees directly into the current schedule layout to demonstrate how origin badges work.

Case 1 — employee from another department in the same store.

Example:
Employee appears inside the current department but has a badge showing the source department.

Example layout:

Олена Ковальчук  
Пекар · 100%   [Каса]

This indicates the employee normally belongs to the "Каса" department but is working a shift in the "Пекарня" department.

Case 2 — employee from another store.

Example:

Марія Лисенко  
Пекар · 100%   [Сільпо №124]

This indicates the employee normally works in another store but temporarily works here.

3. Shift marketplace case.

If a shift was taken from the shift marketplace, keep the existing small label on the shift card:

"На біржі"

This label describes the shift origin, while the employee badge describes the employee origin.

4. Do not change table structure.

Do not create additional sections like "Резерв".
Employees from other departments or stores should remain inside the same department list.

5. Default behavior.

If the employee belongs to the current department and store, do not display any badge.
